Hackathon apps have been deployed.

Services:
  - application-service (port 3001)
  - patient-service (port 3000)
  - order-service (port 8080)

If Ingress is enabled, wait for the external IP and access:
  http://<EXTERNAL-IP>/application
  http://<EXTERNAL-IP>/patient
  http://<EXTERNAL-IP>/order

To get the Ingress IP:
  kubectl get ingress hackathon-apps-ingress
